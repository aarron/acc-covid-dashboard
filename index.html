Alert Levels

Alert-level systems provide a framework to support clear decision-making, improve accountability and communicate with the public to increase healthy behavior change. Learn more about indicators

Alert Level 3: Red

High Risk

 Many cases including community spread, with undetected cases likely.
Limit everyday activities as much as possible.

Alert Level 2: Orange

Moderate Risk

 Increased number of cases with most cases from a known source.
Increase efforts to limit personal exposure.

Alert Level 1: Yellow

Lower Risk

 Cases are rare and contact tracing can be used to control the virus.
Take everyday precautions.
